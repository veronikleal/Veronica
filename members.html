Sensei

Dennis Hoare

Dennis Hoare is listed as 9th degree black belt (9-dan) and has over 40 years of service in martial arts. He began his Isshinryu training in 1962 under Master Don Nagle. Before Master's Nagle's passing, he designated Shihan Hoare to take over the leadership of the school. Hoare is described as active in teaching both the children and adult classes.
